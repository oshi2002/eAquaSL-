About eAqua SL

Our Mission

Our mission is to streamline the aquaculture licensing process in Sri Lanka by providing an efficient, transparent, and user-friendly platform. We aim to empower local farmers and stakeholders by digitizing services offered by NAQDA, ensuring timely license issuance, data-driven decision-making, and sustainable aquaculture practices.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's leading digital platform for aquaculture regulation and support. By closely aligning with NAQDA's objectives, eAqua SL envisions a future where technology bridges the gap between government officers and aquaculture stakeholders, encouraging growth, compliance, and innovation in the fisheries sector.
